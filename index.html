Who Are We.

We are three entrepreneurs, looking forward to quench the thirst of all you readers in this society, with a very simple and lucid idea of sharing books. We see this idea as a pebble thrown on a calm lake of enthusiastic readers and writers, creating a ripple effect of knowledge and community-based bonding. We see this idea as a whetstone for the sharp minds out there; as a base to encourage and build fervent reading and writing. We see this idea as a revolutionary tool in uniting common ideas and ideologies.

Implementation

User profiles will act as a platform for members to display the books they own and desire to share with others, be it novels or reference books. We aim to put forward the books in peoples’ homes and make it available to others. There will be books ranging from a simple maths textbook,to the limited edition DC comic you’ve always wanted to read.

Ensuring aunthenticity in trade

A certain fee will be set by the owner for the book he shares which any member has to deposit to the owner before borrowing that specific book. This deposit ensures that the person lending the book has security in case of damage or loss, and hence will be paid from the fund collected. The deposit on a book will be decided by the book’s owner and will be subject to preset maximums.

Additional plans

The profile will also have a section where the user may write an online blog which will be publicly visible to other users to read and like. Depending on the number of likes and books shared, there will be a “word-smith of the month” who gets special utilities from us for the next month.
